About

Learn what we stand for and who we are.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young individuals by providing opportunities for meaningful community service, leadership development and professional growth while fostering a spirit of inclusivity and international understanding. Through impactful initiatives, collaborative projects and cultural exchange, we aim to develop responsible leaders who contribute positively to society at both local and global levels.

Our Vision

Our Vision To nurture socially responsible leaders who lead with integrity, empathy, and purpose. To inspire positive change in communities through impactful service, innovation, and collaboration. To strengthen global connections by promoting cultural understanding, inclusion, and international fellowship.

Our History

Founded in 2024, we began with a small team passionate about impact. Today we host activities across campus and beyond.

Who can join?

Students of Symbiosis Skills & Professional University who are passionate about service, leadership, and personal growth. Individuals aged 18 years and above with an interest in community service, professional development, and social impact. Students from all departments and backgrounds — no prior experience required. Anyone willing to contribute time, ideas, and energy towards meaningful projects and teamwork. No experience needed — just the passion to serve, lead, and grow.

When do we meet?

Weekly during the semester; see the events calendar.

How do I become a volunteer?

Attend an onboarding session and talk to a coordinator.
